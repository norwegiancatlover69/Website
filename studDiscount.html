Student Discount

How to apply the student discount:

  1. Click on "Order Now" above
  2. Select the wanted items and add them to cart
  3. While in the cart section, select "yes" on "Are you a student?"
  4. Select "Confirm order"
  5. Bring your student ID (Digital or physical) to the selected pick-up spot for confirmation
