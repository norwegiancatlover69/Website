About us

Frydays has redefined the landscape of quick bites with a perfect blend of flavor and conscientious dining. Known for its commitment to locally sourced, high-quality ingredients, Frydays has become a staple for those seeking a balance between indulgence and mindful eating. The menu boasts a diverse range of offerings, from tantalizingly crispy fries to delectable plant-based alternatives, appealing to a wide spectrum of taste preferences. Frydays stands out for its dedication to sustainability and ethical sourcing, cultivating a devoted customer base that values not only the taste but also the integrity of their food choices. The contemporary, inviting atmosphere of Frydays establishments complements the brand's ethos, making it a go-to spot for a satisfying and responsible fast-food experience. Frydays remains a beacon of innovation, proving that deliciousness and social responsibility can coexist in the world of fast-food dining.

Anders Larsen, head chief Oslo

Chef Anders Larsen is leading the charge in the fast-food world with his innovative plant-based creations. Specializing in crafting flavorful dishes without the use of animal products, Larsen has redefined what it means to enjoy a satisfying and eco-conscious meal on the go.

Chef Børge lindeberg, a trailblazer in the fast-food scene, is celebrated for his bold and spicy innovations. With a background rooted in Latin American culinary traditions, Børge brings fiery flavors and a creative flair to his fast-food creations. .
